Your Midwife

Meet Ansie Lima

Ansie is a registered midwife and doula based in Port Elizabeth (Gqeberha). She holds a BCUR Nursing degree from the University of Johannesburg and has years of experience across hospitals, clinics, and home births.

After working alongside some of the best midwives in the country at Genesis Clinic and completing the Sensitive Midwifery Course, Ansie now offers antenatal care, home birth support, hospital doula services, and postnatal and breastfeeding support.

As a mom of two (one hospital birth, one beautiful home birth), she brings both professional expertise and personal experience to every class. She knows what it's like to sit where you're sitting right now.
