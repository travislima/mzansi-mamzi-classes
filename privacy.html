Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 March 2026

Who We Are

Mzansi Mamzi is an antenatal education service operated by Ansie Lima, a registered midwife and doula based in Port Elizabeth (Gqeberha), Eastern Cape, South Africa. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), 2013.

Information We Collect

When you register for our antenatal programme or interact with our website, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Identity information: Full name
  • Contact information: Email address, phone/WhatsApp number
  • Health-related information: Estimated due date (optional, provided voluntarily)
  • Payment information: Processed securely by our payment provider, Yoco. We do not store your card details.
  • Usage data: Anonymous website analytics (pages visited, referral source) collected via Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies or track personal identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process your registration and payment for our antenatal programme
  • To deliver course content via email
  • To add you to the private WhatsApp group for your cohort
  • To send you Zoom session invitations and reminders
  • To issue tax invoices for medical aid claims
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our website and services based on anonymous usage data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: You voluntarily provide your information when registering for our programme.
  • Contractual obligation: Processing is necessary to deliver the services you have paid for.
  • Legitimate interest: Anonymous analytics help us improve our service.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Yoco — Secure payment processing. Subject to Yoco's privacy policy.
  • FormSubmit — Form submission routing via email. No data is stored on their servers.
  • Zoom — Live group sessions. Subject to Zoom's privacy policy.
  • WhatsApp — Group communication. Subject to WhatsApp's privacy policy.
  • Umami — Privacy-focused website analytics. No cookies, no personal data collected.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Registration data: Retained for the duration of your programme participation plus 12 months for follow-up support and record-keeping.
  • Invoice and payment records: Retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law.
  • WhatsApp group messages: Managed within WhatsApp; groups are archived after programme completion.

Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption on our website
  • Secure, PCI-compliant payment processing via Yoco
  • Limited access to personal data (only Ansie Lima has access to registration details)
  • Email obfuscation and spam protection on our contact forms

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies. Our analytics provider, Umami, is a cookie-free, privacy-focused analytics tool. No consent banner is required.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults (expectant parents and their partners). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
